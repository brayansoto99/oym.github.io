Valve for oxygen service

* OyM Valve offers a complete line of oxygen valves, ranging from laboratory requirements to basic oxygen furnace systems for steel. The patented design of its seals ensures an absolutely effective closure.
* Threaded ball valves designed to ANSI Class 150 and ANSI Class 300 are included for this use. The recommended materials from which these valves are manufactured are 316 stainless steel and monel. They can also be supplied in carbon steel, but due to the nature of the oxygen handling hazard, this material is not recommended.
* OyM Valve follows a strict procedure to ensure the cleanliness of all valves specified for oxygen use or service taking into consideration the inherent danger of oxygen reacting with any residual grease, oil or combustible material that may remain in the piping system. All parts are first carefully examined to make sure they are free of burrs, chips, or any foreign material. They are then thoroughly washed, rinsed and then dried in filtered, oil-free air.


valve for hydrogen peroxide

* These K series valves have been developed by OYM VALVE to handle all concentrations of hydrogen peroxide. These valves resist the corrosive effects of peroxide and limit its decomposition to a minimum.
* The valve design includes features that eliminate the possibility of hydrogen peroxide being trapped in any enclosed area where it could create pressure and rupture the valve body. Most important are the special Teflon seats with the characteristic OYM VALVE flexible lip construction. These seats are designed to allow valve relief in the direction of flow at less than 200 psi pressure within the valve body.
* A special assembly procedure is used on hydrogen peroxide valves, including restriction on the use of solvents and lubricants, traces of which can cause decomposition or violent combustion of the peroxide.


Valve for chlorine service

* Their unique design makes OYM Double-Seal ball valves ideal for handling liquid or gaseous chlorine. These valves have satisfactorily covered the requirements of both producers and users of chlorine.
* Most important of all is the patented seal design found in this type of valve. The valve's flexible lip seats not only provide absolutely effective closure, but in the event of excessive pressure buildup in the body, the seats flex, safely venting chlorine to the higher pressure side (clockwise). of flow) from the valve.
* There are additional features that prevent leakage to the atmosphere. Teflon stem seals that seal to the corners prevent leakage through the stem inlet while other potential leak points are eliminated by the integral design of OYM flanges and their highly effective teflon body seals that seal between the body and the body cover of OYM threaded valves.

fire proof valve

* OyM Valve offers several types of ball valves for services with a risk of fire where it is necessary to minimize leakage when the valve is exposed to flammable conditions. Valves that continue to provide effective shutoff even after a fire has occurred are called fireproof valves.
* OYM VALVE fireproof valves are constructed with metal secondary seats. These seats, located behind the polymer seats, are integral sections of the body cover or body insert.
* Under normal conditions the polymer seats provide a perfectly tight seal to the sphere. However, in the event that the polymer seats have been destroyed by fire, the ball will be seated on the secondary metal seat on the side opposite to the direction from which the flow comes, effectively closing the entire valve.

high pressure valve

*Oym VALVE manufactures the threaded end HP style valve specifically designed to handle a wide range of fluids in both the liquid phase and high pressure gas phase.
* The HP valve is made of forged bar whose main characteristic is its mechanical resistance capable of withstanding the most adverse operating conditions. The valve body with this feature prevents the possibility of leakage through union in the body.
* Access to the interior of the valve is from one end and the internal parts are held in place by means of a threaded insert. Additional features make this valve the best selection for both hydraulic and pneumatic high-pressure control systems.
* Its unobstructed flow pattern minimizes pressure drop across the valve, maintaining system pressure requirements. The design of seats and the material of manufacture of the same make the valve operation easy even under the maximum pressure conditions in the line. Because the friction between the ball and the seats is minimal, no lubrication is required. The effectiveness of the HP valve is the result of including the flexible lip seat design which automatically compensates for pressure and temperature changes as well as wear.
