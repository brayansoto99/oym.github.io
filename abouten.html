About OyM Valve

OyM is a company specialized in the manufacture of ball valves and plug valves in carbon steel and stainless steel under the strictest national and international standards.

Quality Politics

The commitment of O Y M Valve, is the manufacture of valves that meet the requirements of our customers, through continuous improvement, based on API Q1 and ISO 9001.

Quality Objetives

Ensure the competence and skill of the personnel involved in the realization of the product while maintaining efficiency at a minimum 66%. Improve the operation of the processes and guarantee their updating by achieving API certification Q1 spec 9th and ISO 9001: 2008. Do not overcome internal rejection by more than 0.75%. Do not exceed the waste by more than 0.5% against the sale. Evaluate and maintain the satisfaction of our customers in a minimum "Good Excellent".

Mission


In OYM Valve we seek to satisfy the needs of our customers by manufacturing valves of good quality and service, based on our processes and continuous improvement. Maintaining the welfare of our employees.

Vision


At OYM Valve we are convinced to become a market leader in the manufacture of valves, using existing methods and technology, consolidating moderate and sustained growth with the efforts of all personnel, to increase the efficiency and effectiveness.

Values


At O y M Valve our values ​​are:
* * Commitment.
* Ethics.
* Respect.
